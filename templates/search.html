Disclosing personal information on the internet can have devastating effects. Social networks can provide attackers with information such as full name, DOB, hometown, relationship information, education, graduation dates, pet names, interests, hobbies, and affiliations. This information can be used in a spear phishing attack to gain further information about a target, as well as crafting targeted password attacks.
